# Models

## Quick positioning

| Model | Best for | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| `seedance-2-fast-preview` | fast tests | cheap, practical, good for prompt iteration |
| `seedance-2-preview` | better Seedance finals | stronger final quality than fast |
| `wan-2.6 1080p` | best value high quality | strong quality-to-cost ratio |
| `veo-3.1` | premium realism | strongest top-tier option |
| `kling-3.0 omni` | ad/commercial output | good control and polished commercial look |

## Decision rules

- choose `seedance-2-fast-preview` for volume testing;
- choose `seedance-2-preview` for better final renders when staying inside Seedance;
- choose `wan-2.6 1080p` for cost-efficient quality;
- choose `veo-3.1` when maximum realism matters more than budget;
- choose `kling-3.0 omni` when the output should feel like polished commercial creative.
